How it works

1

Capture and Curate Survivor Stories

We use conversational speech-optimized AI to interview survivors, capturing their nuanced stories naturally and without questionnaires. This content is then anonymized, screened for dangerous or harmful content, and stored in a secure curated repository of authentic narratives.

2

Discover Personalized Connections

Users explore and discover stories by topics of interest or filter for experiences by demographic similarity or cancer journey stage. Our AI also acts as a gentle guide, proactively suggesting stories that align with a user's current situation.

3

Two-Way Conversational Experience

Users engage in natural, two-way conversations with these lived experiences through a minimalist voice interface that is designed to feel like the same low-effort, natural conversation that they would have with a close friend - providing a secure, private and non-judgmental space for introspection and community connection.
