Authentic peer support for AYA cancer survivors

Lived Lives connects Adolescent & Young Adult (AYA) cancer survivors with authentic, anonymous peer support through conversational AI. Share your story or find solace in the experiences of others through engaging conversations.

Anonymous Conversations

Connect with peers in a safe, anonymous space. Share your experiences without revealing your identity.

From one human to another

Engage in genuine conversations with others who understand your journey. Our AI ensures authentic peer interactions.

Always Available

Access support whenever you need it, 24/7. Our platform is always ready to listen and connect you with peers.

Contribute to the Community

Your story can make a difference. Help others by sharing your lived experience and insights
