Our Mission

Lived Lives is dedicated to democratizing access to authentic peer support for young cancer survivors. Our mission is to empower survivors through the power of storytelling and community.

The Problem

There are over 2.1 million Adolescent & Young Adult (AYA) cancer survivors in the US alone, with over 100,000 new diagnoses each year. Peer support is a critical resource for this community, which faces unique challenges that lead to social isolation and significantly higher rates of depression and anxiety. Peer support is a powerful tool for healing, but it reaches less than 10% of the AYA population. Traditional in-person peer support programs provide valuable support but are inherently limited in scale and accessibility. It's estimated that these services reach less than 10% of the AYA population in the USA, and less than 1% globally.

Our Solution

LivedLives is a conversational AI platform that uses technology to bridge this gap. We democratize access to authentic peer support for AYA cancer survivors by providing a scalable, affordable, and on-demand digital alternative that is designed to reach the 90-99% who have no access. Our platform ensures privacy and psychological safety, allowing survivors to connect with the lived experiences of others who have gone through similar journeys and find the support they need, when they need it.

Core Values

At Lived Lives, we are guided by empathy, resilience, and hope. We believe in the power of shared experiences and the strength found in community. Our platform is built on a foundation of privacy, accuracy, and respect for every survivor's journey.

Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every young cancer survivor feels supported, understood, and empowered to navigate their unique challenges. We strive to be the leading platform for peer support, fostering connections that inspire hope and resilience.
